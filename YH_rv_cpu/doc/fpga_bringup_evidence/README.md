# FPGA Bring-Up Evidence

Store real-board evidence for `YH_rv_cpu` here after the Nexys A7-100T arrives.

Recommended layout:

- `YYYY-MM-DD/bitstream/`
- `YYYY-MM-DD/reports/`
- `YYYY-MM-DD/serial/`
- `YYYY-MM-DD/photos/`
- `YYYY-MM-DD/video/`

Minimum expected contents per bring-up session:

- copied bitstream path and report paths
- at least one Vivado screenshot
- at least one UART terminal screenshot or full serial log
- at least one LED photo or short video
- board serial number / board revision note

Do not claim board-ready closure until the checklist in
`../fpga_bringup_checklist.md` is completed with real hardware evidence.
